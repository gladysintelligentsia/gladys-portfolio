How Electric Vehicles Work

3 Steps to Zero Emissions

1. Energy Storage: Electricity is stored in a large lithium-ion battery pack, usually located at the base of the car.
2. Power Conversion: When you press the accelerator, the controller gathers energy from the battery and sends it to the electric motor.
3. Propulsion: The motor uses that electricity to create magnetic fields that rotate the motor, which turns the wheels via a simple transmission.
