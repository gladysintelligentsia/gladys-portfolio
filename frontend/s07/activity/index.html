Electric Vehicles (EVs)

How do EVs work?

  1. Storage: Electricity is stored in a large battery pack, usually located at the bottom of the vehicle.
  2. Conversion: An electric motor takes that energy and converts it into mechanical power.
  3. Movement: The motor rotates the wheels via a transmission, propelling the car forward without any exhaust.
